Guatapé

Guatapé is an Andean resort town in northwest Colombia, east of Medellín.

Nightlife

Nightlife in Medellín is mostly about drinking and dancing Salsa, Merengue, Bachata, or Reggaeton.

Paragliding

Feel the rush of adrenaline as you soar high above the Andes and take in views.

Pueblito Paisa

A replica of a typical turn of the century town with a traditional stone fountain in the town square.

Parque Arví

Arví Park is both an ecological nature preserve and Pre-Hispanic archeological site.

Plaza Botero

Surrounded by the Museum of Antioquia, this park displays 23 sculptures by Fernando Botero.

Metrocable

This gondola lift was system designed to reach some of the informal settlements on the steep hills of city.

Parque Lleras

The zona rosa of Medellín. A place where you can find restaurants, cafés, bars, and clubs.

Comuna 13

Once one of the most violent areas in the city, now known for its street art, boutique and outdoor escalators.

Parque Explora

An interactive science museum, and Home to the largest freshwater aquarium in South America.
